Upward Evaluation Agreement

Please review the terms and conditions before proceeding with the evaluation

Terms & Conditions
  1. Purpose: This upward evaluation system collects feedback from faculty members to improve coordination effectiveness and institutional management.
  2. Professional Conduct: Provide honest, constructive, and professional feedback. Avoid personal attacks or inappropriate comments. Evaluations should focus on professional performance and leadership effectiveness.
  3. Academic Integrity: Your evaluation should reflect your genuine experience and observations working with the coordinator.
  4. One Submission: You can only submit one evaluation per coordinator. Once submitted, evaluations are final and cannot be modified.
  5. Data Usage: Feedback is used for coordinator development, administrative improvement, and institutional research. Results may be shared with relevant administrators.
  6. Confidentiality: While responses are treated confidentially, administrators may be able to identify responses based on context. Be professional in all feedback.
  7. Consequences: Misuse of the system, including providing false information or inappropriate feedback, may result in disciplinary action.
Privacy Policy
  1. Data Collection: We collect evaluation responses, coordinator information, faculty information, and submission timestamps.
  2. Confidentiality: Your responses are kept confidential and used solely for professional development and administrative improvement.
  3. Anonymity Limitations: Individual responses may be identifiable to administrators, especially in small departments. Responses are aggregated for coordinator reports when possible.
  4. Data Storage: Data is stored securely in encrypted databases and accessed only by authorized personnel (administrators and system managers).
  5. Data Retention: Evaluation data is retained for institutional research, performance reviews, and accreditation purposes.
  6. No Sharing: Your evaluation data will not be shared with third parties without proper authorization or legal requirement.
  7. Your Rights: You have the right to know how your data is used. Contact the administration for questions about data handling.
You must agree to the terms and conditions before proceeding.
